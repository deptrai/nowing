UX Design Directions: Nowing Institutional Terminal

Exploring how to integrate Epic 10 (Smart Money, Heatmaps, Tokenomics) into the RAG workflow.

Direction 1: The Dense Terminal (Bloomberg-lite)

Focus: Maximum data density, dark mode default, multi-panel layouts. The chat is a command line at the bottom.

Pros: Institutional feel, shows everything at once. Cons: Steep learning curve, intimidating for retail users.

Direction 2: The Clean Analyst (Messari Style)

Focus: Report-centric, reading-optimized. Dashboards and reports are the main view; AI chat is a floating assistant.
