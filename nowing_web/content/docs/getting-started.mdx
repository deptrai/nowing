Nowing is a hosted AI knowledge platform for teams — there is nothing to install. Create an account and you are ready to go.

## 1. Create your account

Head to the [sign-up page](/register) and create an account with email or Google. Your first workspace is created automatically.

## 2. Add your knowledge

Bring your knowledge into Nowing in one of two ways:

- **Upload documents** — drag in PDFs, docs, slides, images, and more.
- **Connect a source** — link tools like Slack, Google Drive, Notion, Confluence, and dozens more so Nowing stays in sync automatically.

## 3. Ask anything

Open a chat and ask questions about your knowledge base. Nowing answers with citations back to the original sources, and can generate reports, podcasts, and videos from what it finds.

Models are managed for you — there are no API keys to configure. Pick a model from the chat header, or let Auto mode choose the best one for each request.

## 4. Invite your team

Workspaces are collaborative. Invite teammates from **Settings → Team**, assign roles (Owner, Admin, Editor, Viewer), and work together in real time with shared chats and comments.
